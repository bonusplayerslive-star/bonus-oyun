Tutarı Girmeli: Göndermek istediği USDT miktarını yazmalı.

Network Selection: Make sure you select the network you're interested in from the top (e.g., BNB Smart Chain).

Asset Selection: Click on USDT from the list.

Send Button: * On the screen that opens, paste the address you provided, starting with 0x..., into the "Address" field.

Amount Entry: Enter the amount of USDT you wish to send.

Confirmation: Press the "Next" and then the "Confirm" buttons.

Gas Fee (Transaction Fee): To make a transfer on MetaMask, you must have some BNB coins in your wallet (to pay the fee):

For BNB Smart Chain: You must have some BNB.
